Membership

We work with children 5-13 at any level. They work and learn at their peace and often work in projects and teams to encourage working with peers.

To fit your and your child's needs you have the option to pre-pay for an entire year for a better price, half a year, monthly, or as you go (per class). We offer sibling and military discounts and seasonal specials.

Coding is one skill we teach, but socializing and teamwork are always part of it. Help your child to unlock their full potential, make friends, have fun, and invest in their future.

Schedule

Every meeting is after school. We have meetings Monday through Friday, every hour from 4 pm - 7 pm, and your child can attend the center or meet online from the comfort of your home. Saturday meetings are every hour from 9 am - 3 pm.

To enroll, fill out the form above, and a representative will contact you in less than 24 hours to schedule a free class and a tour of the facility so you and your child can love this program as much as we do.

We are so excited to hear from you!
